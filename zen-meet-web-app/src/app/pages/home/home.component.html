ZenMeet
Online Real-Time
Multi-Communication Platform
Create meeting rooms, video and audio calls, send messages, share your screen and use interactive features like reactions and raise hand with multiple participants on ZenMeet.
Disclaimer: This application is inspired by platforms like Microsoft Teams, Zoom, and Google Meet and is intended for personal experimentation and learning only. ZenMeet is a real-time communication platform designed for seamless and interactive online meetings. The platform uses WebRTC for video and audio calls, public STUN servers for establishing connection between peers and custom Pub-Sub channels for messaging and sharing connections details. For more details, check out ZenMeet System Design Blog.
